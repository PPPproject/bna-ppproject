PPP Project

Intro

To combat job loss spurred by 2020's COVID-19 pandemic, the U.S. Small Business Administration designed the Paycheck Protection Program (PPP), designed to provide incentive to small businesses in the form of loans. This dashboard aims to visualize PPP loan distribution in California, with summary statistics by county.
